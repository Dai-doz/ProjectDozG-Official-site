What is ProjectDozG?

A custom ROM based on One UI 8.5 is a modified version of Samsung's official software. It retains the core look and feel of One UI but adds new features, performance enhancements, and customizations that aren't available on the standard firmware.

Key Features

ProjectDozG Core

A powerful in-ROM control center : system monitor , performance modes , CPU/GPU temps , RAM monitor , battery stats , display tuning , and more — all in real time.

Neutron+ Kernel

Custom Neutron+ Kernel based on Rsuntk backported source code with ProjectDozG's own DVFS implementation, tuned for maximum battery life, stability, and peak performance.

Extreme Privacy

Bootloader Unlocking

Follow these core setup requirements strictly to ensure safety during the installation phase.

1
Enable Developer options in settings.
2
Plug the phone to the PC.
3
Keep pressing the button of volume up and volume down.
4
Now keep pressed up button to get in Bootloader Unlock Mode.
5
Press vol + to unlock bootloader.

ROM Install Steps

Flashing instructions prepared for standard recovery steps.

1
Make sure you're on the latest Stock firmware (Check on SamFW).
2
Make sure your OEM Bootloader is Unlocked.
3
Download OFOX/TWRP on Official site . Download Vbmeta.tar .
4
Put device in Download Mode.
5
Load OFOX/TWRP TAR file in AP, and under Odin Options, untick Auto Reboot.
6
Force reboot by holding POWER + Vol Down.
7
While on OFOX/TWRP, go to flash repartition.zip then reboot recovery and wipe all partitions.
8
Put OFOX/TWRP in Sideload mode.
9
Sideload the ROM from the computer.
10
Before restart go to wipe and format data.
11
Reboot and enjoy!
